RESCHEDULE AND CANCELLATION POLICY

You may reschedule or cancel your testing center registration without incurring an additional fee with a minimum of 72 hours advance notice of the scheduled start time of your exam. Please note: 72 hours is not three business days; it is 72 hours prior to the scheduled exam start time.

Reschedules and cancellations more than 72 hours before your exam start time can be completed through your test taker account in Webassessor free of charge. To reschedule or cancel within 72 hours of your scheduled exam start time, you will be charged a $75 fee. This can be completed through your test taker account, but a fee will be assessed (instructions below).

If you do not complete the exam at the scheduled time and did not contact the Salesforce Certification team in advance of your exam start date and time, you will be considered a no-show and charged the full exam fee.

Instructions on how to reschedule or cancel your exam and additional information about the reschedule and cancellation policy can be found on our website: Exam Reschedule and Cancellation Policy.
